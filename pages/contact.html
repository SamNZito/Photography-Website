About Me

As a skilled multimedia creator and videographer, I specialize in crafting visually stunning and engaging content that makes executives, leaders, brands, and companies look their best. Whether it's recording interviews, creating promotional videos, or producing Zoom meetings, I have the expertise to handle all the details and deliver top-notch results, saving busy professionals valuable time and reducing stress.

In addition to my video production skills, I have experience in logo design, drone flying, photography, and video/photo editing, all of which help me create impactful visual content. My expertise in studio setup and production enables me to create the perfect environment for recording and producing videos, ensuring a seamless and professional experience.

I'm passionate about helping clients tell their stories and showcase their brand in the best possible light. Let's work together to create content that captures attention, engages audiences, and drives results!

My journey began as an amateur photographer, driven by a passion for capturing moments through the lens. I honed my skills and later joined Beachmont as a Media Coordinator and Videographer. I am currently pursuing my studies in digital media, I am expected to graduate in 2027.
